Building Information

  • Fine Arts Center (FAC)
    Art based classes are held here. Town hall and bigger events are hosted here.
  • Student Center
    Place to study. Student organizations often meet here. The Lucy Rose Center is located on the lower level. The Lucy Rose Center is our career readiness center. The Cafe is on the upper level.
  • Gramley Library
    Resources and study spaces. The Writing Center as well as the Nest are located on upper levels. The only building with an elevator.
  • Gramley Dorm
    Residential hall for students. For upperclassmen, predominantly athletes.
  • Clewell Dorm
    Residential hall for students. For first-year students only.
  • South Dorm
    Residential hall for students. For first-year honor students.
  • 1772 Dorm
    Residential hall for upperclassmen.
  • Bonson Dorm
    Residential hall for students, has space for three roommates.
  • Science Building
    Labs and lecture classrooms.
  • Main Hall
    Lecture classrooms, professor offices. Public safety is below the porch .
  • Refectory (RAT)
    Buffet style dining hall.
